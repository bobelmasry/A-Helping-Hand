Our Story:

When starting the enterprise, our team noticed the heartbreaking widespread struggles visible within egyptian society and issues within its economy that have affected an outstanding amount of the countries population for years, with rising issues with those who are disabled finding employment and attempting to provide for themselves in difficult situations, A helping hand strives to aid in dismantling negative and flawed stigmas around people with disabilities through our constantly developing movement doing whatever possible to make a change.
